Common Questions

FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from our customers.

How fresh is the coffee when it arrives?
We roast to order. Your bag ships within 24–48 hours of roasting and arrives at peak freshness — never sitting in a warehouse collecting stale air.
Do you offer subscriptions?
Yes! Subscribe to any product and save 15% on every order. Choose your frequency: weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Cancel or pause anytime with one click.
What is your returns policy?
We stand behind our coffee 100%. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 30 days and we'll replace your order or issue a full refund. No questions asked.
Do you offer wholesale pricing?
Absolutely. We work with cafes, restaurants, and offices of all sizes. Select "Wholesale / Bulk Order" in the form above and we'll send you our pricing guide within 24 hours.
