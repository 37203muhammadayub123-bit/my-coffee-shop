Who We Are

A Passion for
Perfect Coffee.

Started in a Brooklyn apartment. Built on an obsession with sourcing, roasting, and delivering the world's finest coffees.

Our Mission

Sourced Ethically.
Roasted Precisely.

We started Brew & Co because we believed specialty coffee had a transparency problem. Consumers deserved to know exactly where their coffee came from — and farmers deserved to be paid fairly for exceptional work.

Today we partner directly with 38 small-scale farms across 12 countries, paying 30–50% above fair trade rates and investing in community infrastructure in every region we source from.

Our Journey

Ten Years
of Craft.

2015
The Beginning
Marcus and Elena began roasting in their Brooklyn apartment, selling bags to friends who couldn't get enough of the flavours.
2017

Opened our first dedicated roastery space in Brooklyn with a team of four passionate coffee obsessives.
2020
Going National
Launched online shipping across the US. Reached 10,000 loyal customers within six months of going digital.
2025
Global Sourcing

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01
Sustainability First
Eco-friendly packaging, 100% carbon offset through verified environmental programs, and a commitment to zero waste at our roastery by 2026.
02
Farmer First
We pay 30–50% above fair trade rates and invest directly in school and healthcare infrastructure in every farming community we source from.
03
Radical Transparency
We publish the farm name, harvest date, processing method, and tasting notes on every product. You always know exactly what you're drinking.
04
Obsessive Quality
Only the top 5% of lots we cup ever make it into a Brew & Co bag. Our master roasters refine each profile across dozens of test batches.
